Quarterly Sales Analysis

This was a guided project. In this I develop a dynamic and comprehensive eCommerce Sales Dashboard using Power BI to provide actionable insights.

The dashboard encompasses key metrics such as

  1. State wise revenue.
  2. Quantity based on the category as well as Payment mode
  3. Amount as per the customer
  4. Profit per month
  5. Profit based on sub category

The approach that I followed:

  1. Utilizeed data visualizations, including bar graphs, cards, pie chart to present information in a clear and intuitive manner.
  2. Implement filters and interactive elements to allow users to explore sales data based on time periods.
The goal is to enhance decision-making, identifies growth opportunities, and optimize the overall eCommerce sales strategy.
