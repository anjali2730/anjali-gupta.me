Stock Market Analysis

Developed a comprehensive power bi dashboard for stock market analysis incorporating key financial indicators and historical stock performance.

Key points that I highlighted:

  1. Highest closing as per year
  2. Grand total of the company per year
  3. Lowest stock rates for the company
  4. Highest turnover till date

Approach followed.

  1. Cleansed the data to remove duplicates, transform data into a suitable format for analysis
  2. Created compelling visualizations such as line charts for stock trends. Leverage Power BI's visualization options to convey information effectively.
  3. Implemented slicers, filters, and drill-down functionalities to make the dashboard interactive.

Tools used

  1. MS Excel
  2. Power BI
